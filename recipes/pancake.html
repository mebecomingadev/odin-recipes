Pancake

Description

A pancake is a breakfast dish, a flat cake that's made by pouring batter into a hot pan and frying it on both sides. ... Most pancakes begin as a mixture of flour, eggs, milk, and butter, are fried on a greased griddle, and are circular in shape.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, or vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Grab a griddle or large nonstick skillet
  2. Preheat and lightly grease your pan.
  3. Use a simple ratio to make batter
  4. Don't overbeat the batter
  5. Cook each pancake until bubbles form on top
  6. Add toppings before you flip
  7. Flip and let cook for another minute
